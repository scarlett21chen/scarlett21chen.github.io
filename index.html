AR Pokemon Go Spinoff

A2-GO is a project that was inspired by games like Pokemon Go to create an augmented reality video game for mobile devices. In this game, the player is tasked with planting seeds in order to grow trees, allowing them to earn in-game money. However, while playing the game, their trees can suddenly be attacked by squirrels which they must defeat. Along with this, they can use their money to buy more seeds, and walk to different landmarks to unlock new seeds. Overall, this game provides an interactive experience while requiring a physical aspect to it.
